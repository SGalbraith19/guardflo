Make a financial decision

One API call enforces financial policy for automated systems.

Request

import requests

response = requests.post(
"https://api.guardflo.com/api/v1/decide",
headers={"Authorization":"Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"},
json={
"transaction_id":"txn_123",
"amount":5000,
"currency":"USD",
"merchant":"Stripe",
"nonce":"abc123"
})

Deterministic financial enforcement

Deterministic Decisions

GuardFlo evaluates financial transactions against strict policy rules. No AI. No probabilistic models. Every decision is reproducible.

Tamper-Evident Ledger

Every decision is stored in a cryptographic append-only ledger with hash chaining and digital signatures.

Automation Safety

Prevent automated systems from executing financial actions that violate policy or exceed limits.

Prevent Financial Automation Failures

Duplicate payouts, policy violations, and automation errors are common in distributed financial workflows. GuardFlo acts as a deterministic enforcement layer before financial actions execute.
